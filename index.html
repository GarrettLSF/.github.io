Our Proven 4-Step Process

No surprises, just results. Our structured approach ensures every project is predictable, efficient, and transparent from start to finish.

1

Pre-Install Coordination

Comprehensive site review, logistics planning, and timeline confirmation with your PM. We identify potential challenges before they become problems and coordinate with all stakeholders to ensure smooth execution.

2

Professional Installation

Our uniformed, expert team arrives on time with the right tools and experience. We follow school protocols, maintain clean work areas, and execute installations with precision and efficiency.

3

Quality Control Walkthrough

Detailed punch list review with your representative to ensure every piece meets specifications. We address any concerns immediately and don't consider the job complete until you're 100% satisfied.

4

Final Sign-Off & Cleanup

Project completion report, warranty documentation, and site left cleaner than we found it. You receive all necessary paperwork and peace of mind for your client handoff.
