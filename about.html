Built From Experience. Designed For You.

Elite Install was born from a simple but powerful idea: furniture dealers deserve an installation partner who is as committed to their success as they are. We are not just a crew for hire; we are a strategic partner founded to solve the exact problems we faced in our own careers.

From Project Manager to Your Most Trusted Partner

Before founding Elite Install, our leader spent years as a senior project manager for educational and commercial furniture dealerships.

He lived the daily struggle you know all too well: the missed deadlines from unreliable subs, the budget overruns from inefficiency, and the constant brand damage caused by unprofessional crews on-site. The stress of managing installations often overshadowed the satisfaction of a completed project.

He knew there had to be a better way. A partnership built on transparency, accountability, and a deep respect for the dealer's reputation.

So, he built it.

Elite Install was meticulously designed to be the installation company he always wished he could hire. Our entire process is filtered through the lens of a project manager. We anticipate challenges, communicate proactively, and treat every single project with the urgency and professionalism it deserves.

Our Core Commitments

Our values aren't just words on a page; they are the guiding principles for every action we take on every job site. This is our promise to you.

Unwavering Reliability

Your schedule is our guide. We approach every project with thorough planning and logistical foresight to ensure your timeline is respected and your peace of mind is protected.

Radical Professionalism

Our uniformed crew understands they are ambassadors for your brand. We operate with courtesy and respect, maintaining a clean and orderly site to reflect the quality you promise.

Dedicated Expertise

We get it right the first time. We do not use temporary labor. Our full-time, dedicated team has the training and tools necessary to execute installations efficiently, minimizing punch lists and return trips.

Proactive Communication

You will always be informed. We establish a clear rhythm of communication and provide a single point of contact, so you never have to guess the status of your project.
